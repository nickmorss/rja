Burning Man • 7 Events • 5 Continents

Exploring Community
in a Disconnected World

Roxane Jessi — author, adventurer, and global citizen — set out to experience seven Burning Man events firsthand, seeking the culture that creates community where none exists.

Follow the Adventures

"These communities are creating something the world desperately needs."

Having spent a career working on Congolese police reform, Iraq counterterrorism, and women's rights in Africa, Roxane Jessi has witnessed firsthand how broken communities can become — and what it takes to rebuild them.

In 2018 she set out on a different kind of mission: to travel to seven Burning Man events across the globe, from the Nevada desert to the Israeli Negev, the Australian Outback to the Spanish Monegros, in search of the communities rewriting the rules of human connection.

The Book

Part travel memoir, part social inquiry — Roxane's book chronicles her journey through the world's Burning Man Regional events, exploring how thousands of people are building radical communities in some of the most unforgiving environments on earth.

From the dusty shores of the Negev Desert at Midburn to the eucalyptus forests of Western Australia at Blazing Swan, each event offers a window into a culture determined to reimagine the world from the ground up.
